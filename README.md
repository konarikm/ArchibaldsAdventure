# Archibald's Adventure

Welcome to the Forest of Oblivion. A place where sunlight never reaches and time is measured only by how long you can stay alive.

Archibald used to be one of the most gifted students at the Magic Academy. He specialized in a rare and highly unstable form of energy - known as amaranthine magic. Everyone warned him that these forces were not to be trifled with. But Archibald wanted more. During one complex experiment, a miscalculation occurred. A magical flash tore reality apart, and Archibald plummeted into the void.

He woke up here. In the Forest.

The forest is alive, and it despises intruders. Archibald soon discovered that toxic creatures born from the rotting woods lurk at every turn - whether it's the lazily crawling Moss Slimes or the lightning-fast, mutated orange Feral Thornbacks. His only weapon is his own devastating pink magic.

A way out of the forest exists. It lies in ancient spatial rifts - portals. But when Archibald reaches them, he finds them dark, dormant, and lifeless. To awaken them with his magic and open the path forward, he must risk his life in hostile territory to collect lost Fragments of Sun Gold. Only when he gathers enough of them in a given area will the portal illuminate, allowing Archibald to step one pace closer to home. This isn't just an escape; it's a fight for survival.
